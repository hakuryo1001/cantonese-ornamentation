\chapter{pgfhan ornament catalog}

This document showcases the \textbf{pgfhan} family from the vendored
\texttt{pgfornament} package: 78 Chinese traditional motifs drawn by
Chennan Zhang and converted for \LaTeX{} by LianTze Lim.

\section{Basic usage}

Load the package with the \texttt{pgfhan} object (already configured in
\texttt{preamble/pgfornament.tex}):

\begin{verbatim}
\usepackage[object=pgfhan]{pgfornament}
\pgfornament[width=2cm, color=SeaGreen]{59}
\end{verbatim}

Example rendered at catalog scale:

\begin{center}
  \pgfornament[width=2cm, color=SeaGreen]{59}
\end{center}

\section{Symmetry options}

Horizontal, vertical, and central symmetry are available via the
\texttt{symmetry} key:

\begin{center}
\begin{tikzpicture}
  \node at (0,0) {\pgfornament[width=1.5cm]{40}};
  \node at (3,0) {\pgfornament[width=1.5cm, symmetry=h]{40}};
  \node at (6,0) {\pgfornament[width=1.5cm, symmetry=v]{40}};
  \node at (9,0) {\pgfornament[width=1.5cm, symmetry=c]{40}};
\end{tikzpicture}
\end{center}

\noindent
From left to right: none, \texttt{symmetry=h}, \texttt{symmetry=v},
\texttt{symmetry=c}.
